Seraab Valley

Project description

Dry Fruits have been a part of our diet and our culture since times immemorial. Along with the umpteen health benefits that they offer, dry fruits are also an excellent source of vital nutrients and healthy oils that help reduce the risk of chronic diseases. A long shelf life, ease of storage and transportation make them an inherent part of every dietary recommendation. Seraab Valley is our endeavor to deliver this goodness right at your doorstep. Building on our two decades of flourishing wholesale operations, Seraab Valley are now foraying into the online space for offering dry fruits online. Seraab Valley aspire to reach out to consumers across the country with an exciting array of Kashmiri Dry Fruits with unmatched assortments and mouth-watering variations. Seraab valley offer more than 40 different varieties of dry fruits and nuts, starting from the traditional and the classic, such as Walnuts, Almonds, Saffron, Apricots and much more. Seraab Valley's dry fruits and nuts are sourced from the top-notch quality growers, and cleaned, sorted and packaged manually to ensure that their freshness and flavor is well preserved. We meticulously comply with the standards of Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) at our cleaning and packaging facility. We also follow a stringent quality control practices. This helps us ensure that we deliver dry fruits and nuts of premium quality and taste.
